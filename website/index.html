Status language

Six states, no ambiguity

Official
Generated from repository-owned policies and host bindings.
Signed
Catalog content is bound to a trusted maintainer signature.
Recommended
Trusted signature plus current canonical evaluation gates pass.
Experimental
Valid to inspect or test; live evidence or trusted signature is not yet published.
Custom
Locally compiled from user-owned policies or bindings.
Unverified
Integrity, signature, or evidence checks have not established publishable trust.

Local-first by design. A catalog outage never affects installed packages or active projects.
